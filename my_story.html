My story

As a teacher

Monica

Parts of my lovely students

My undergraduate major is Chinese language and literature, but I found that I like children very much and also like mathematics, so after graduation, I chose to be a math teacher.I worked as a math teacher for three years from 2015 to 2018.I really enjoyed my time as a teacher. I enjoyed the innocence and kindness of children and the joy of sharing knowledge. Of course, I have also gained the love of many children, and we have kept in touch until now, and I have been watching their growth. Of course, I also hope that the computer knowledge I have learned now can be used in the field of education.
